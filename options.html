Lingvo live Omnibox Options

This is an unofficial extension for lingvolive.com brought to life by the untimely death of slovari.yandex.ru, which I miss to this day.

Omnibox keyword is l for lingvo. You can choose any two of the available languages to translate between, although Russian has the most dictionaries. If you notice a ♪ in a suggestion, that means you can hit Alt+Enter on it and someone's charming voice will pronounce that word for you.
