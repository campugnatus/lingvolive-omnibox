Lingvo live Omnibox Options

This is an unofficial extension for lingvolive.ru brought to life by the untimely death of slovari.yandex.ru, for which we all agreed to grieve immensely.

Omnibox keyword is "l". You can choose any two of the available languages to translate between, although Russian has the most dictionaries. If you notice a little note in a suggestion, you can hit Alt+Enter on it and someone's charming voice will pronounce that word for you.
